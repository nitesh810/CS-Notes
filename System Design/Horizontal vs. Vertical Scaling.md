#### Horizontal Scaling
1. It is referred to as Scale-out 
2. More costly
3. More fault tolerant / reliable
4. Difficult to maintain
5. Slower

#### Vertical Scaling
1. It is referred to as Scale-up
2. Less costly
3. Single point of failure
4. Easier to maintain
5. Faster
